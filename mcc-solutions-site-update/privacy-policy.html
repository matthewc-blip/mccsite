Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

MCC Solutions LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website mcc-solutionsnj.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site or contact us about our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you contact us, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number submitted via our contact form
  • Service inquiries and appointment details
  • Communications sent via text, email, WhatsApp, or phone
  • Information required to perform notary or bookkeeping services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule appointments
  • Perform notary, loan signing, and bookkeeping services
  • Send appointment confirmations and follow-up communications
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements as a licensed notary and financial services provider
  • Improve our website and services

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With signing platforms, title companies, or escrow officers as required to complete loan signing appointments
  • With accounting or tax software providers (such as Intuit QuickBooks) to perform bookkeeping services
  • When required by law, court order, or government authority
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of MCC Solutions LLC or others

Notary Records

As a licensed New Jersey Notary Public, we are required by state law to maintain a notary journal. Information recorded in our notary journal is kept in accordance with New Jersey notary regulations and is not shared except as required by law.

Contact Form & Communications

When you submit our contact form or reach out via text, email, or WhatsApp, your information is received directly by MCC Solutions LLC and is not stored on third-party servers beyond standard email and messaging infrastructure.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use basic analytics tools to understand visitor traffic and improve site performance. We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to advertisers. If we implement analytics tools, they will be disclosed here.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information using industry-standard practices.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Notary records are retained in accordance with New Jersey state law. Bookkeeping records are retained as required by IRS and applicable accounting standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party services including Calendly, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services and encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
